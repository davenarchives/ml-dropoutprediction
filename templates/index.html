Dataset Info

Student Dropout Dataset

Source: Kaggle. The dataset contains enrollment, demographic, socioeconomic, and academic indicators used to classify whether a student is likely to drop out or continue.

Target Variable

Binary target: Dropout (1) vs Non-Dropout (0).

Feature Families

Inputs include academic performance, financial status, family background, course enrollment, and demographic context.
